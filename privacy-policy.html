Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18/04/2026

Last Updated: 18/04/2026

Bella Flor Jewellery is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit bellaflorjewellery.co.uk or purchase from us. We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who We Are

Bella Flor Jewellery is a UK-based handmade jewellery business specialising in bespoke, hand-knotted cord bracelets. For any privacy-related queries, you can contact us at contact@bellaflorjewellery.co.uk.

Information We Collect

  1. Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  2. Order Information: Details of products, custom specifications, and payment records.
  3. Communications: Messages via contact forms, email, WhatsApp, or Instagram.
  4. Technical Information: IP address, cookies, and device info for analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  1. Process and fulfil your orders and commissions.
  2. Communicate order and delivery updates.
  3. Send marketing communications (where consented).
  4. Improve our website and customer experience.
  5. Comply with legal obligations.

How We Share Your Information

  1. Delivery Services: Royal Mail or couriers to dispatch orders.
  2. Payment Processors: Secure third-party platforms (e.g., PayPal, Stripe).
  3. Legal Authorities: Where required by law.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also withdraw consent for marketing at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at contact@bellaflorjewellery.co.uk.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
